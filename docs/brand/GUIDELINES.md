# Hexendrum Brand Manual  
*Visual Identity Guidelines*  

---

## 1. Brand Essence  
- **Name Meaning:** *Hexendrum* (Hexen = witches, Drum = ritual rhythm)  
- **Core Values:** Ritual • Darkness • Music • Enchantment  
- **Tone of Voice:** Mysterious, poetic, modern, respectful of folklore  

---

## 2. Logo  
- **Primary Logo:** Gothic-inspired wordmark + ritual drum emblem  
- **Secondary Logo:** Wordmark only  
- **Symbol/Emblem:** Circular rune-drum, used as app icon or watermark  

**Usage Rules**  
- Maintain clear space equal to height of the “H” around logo  
- Minimum size: 24px (digital) or 15mm (print)  
- Logo must always appear in brand colours (black, crimson, silver, or green accents)  

---

## 3. Colour Palette  

**Primary Colours**  
- Obsidian Black — `#0B0B0D`  
- Blood Crimson — `#7A1C1C`  

**Secondary Colours**  
- Ash Grey — `#B1B1B3`  
- Moonlit Silver — `#DDE1E6`  

**Accent**  
- Witchlight Green — `#3FAE7C`  

---

## 4. Typography  

**Logo & Titles**  
- *Cinzel Decorative* or *UnifrakturCook* (Gothic-inspired)  

**Body & UI**  
- *Inter* or *Montserrat* (clean sans-serif for readability)  

**Hierarchy**  
- H1: Gothic (36pt+)  
- H2: Gothic (24–30pt)  
- Body: Sans-serif (14–16pt digital, 10–12pt print)  

---

## 5. Graphic Elements  
- **Motifs:** Runes, ritual drums, botanical drawings  
- **Textures:** Parchment, stone, smoke overlays  
- **Photography Style:** Dark, moody, high contrast, mystical lighting  

---

## 6. Application Examples  
- **App Icon:** Rune-drum glowing in Witchlight Green over black  
- **UI:**  
  - Dark mode default  
  - Active elements highlighted in crimson/green  
  - Library displayed with clean grid, rune dividers between sections  
- **Website:** Hero section with ritual circle animation  
- **Merch:** Shirts, stickers, and posters with emblem  

---

## 7. Do’s & Don’ts  

**Do**  
- ✔ Keep logo on dark backgrounds  
- ✔ Use Witchlight Green only as accent/glow  
- ✔ Pair Gothic titles with Sans-serif body text  

**Don’t**  
- ✘ Stretch or distort the logo  
- ✘ Use bright/pastel colours  
- ✘ Overload visuals with too many motifs — less is more  

---

## 8. Voice & Messaging  
- **Tone:** Darkly poetic, modern, ritualistic  
- **Examples:**  
  - “Summon your sound.”  
  - “The rhythm of witches, the pulse of music.”  
  - “Hexendrum: Where ritual meets resonance.”
